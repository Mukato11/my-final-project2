Welcome to Georgia

Georgia is a transcontinental country located at the intersection of Eastern Europe and Western Asia, known for its diverse landscapes, rich history, and unique culture, including being the birthplace of wine. It is situated in the Caucasus region, bordered by Russia, Turkey, Armenia, and Azerbaijan, and has a Black Sea coastline. The capital and largest city is Tbilisi, and the official language is Georgian.
